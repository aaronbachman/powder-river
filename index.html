Welcome

Powder River Outfitters owns and leases several hundred square miles of prime hunting land in Southeastern Montana. The beautiful landscape combined with our knowledge of the area will result in an experience of a lifetime. Montana is home to many of the largest Whitetail, Mule Deer, Antelope, and Turkeys in the world. Powder River Outfitter has over 20 years experience and our guides will provide you with the absolute best opportunity for a trophy hunt. We are an equal opportunity employer.

Facilities

Our camp is rustic, but comfortable. Powder River Outfitters is family owned and operated. We have been under new ownership since February 2014. Our exceptional guides have been with us every step of the way, and make Powder River Outfitters what it is today. We furnish all the equipment, except personal effects, necessary to make your hunt successful and enjoyable. Our trailers are heated, with electric lights, and comfortable mattresses. All meals are prepared by our cook and special requests can be made with prior notice. We care for your meat, trophy antlers, and capes as if they were our own. The client covers the cost of freight, taxidermy, and meat processing. If you wound an animal, it is yours and every attempt will be made to recover the animal, therefore no other animals will be harvested.

Company Pledge

It is our pledge put in the extra effort to provide you with a hunting experience of a lifetime. Each hunter is individually guided to assure you have a successful and memorable trip. Your satisfaction is our number one priority and we will do everything in our power to achieve that goal. We have plenty of references and are proud to have aired on several television shows. You will be satisfied with your decision to choose Powder River Outfitters so please contact us and reserve your spot today, space is limited.
